STATEMENT OF PURPOSE

The Objective

To document, amplify, and archive the systemic failures of the medical industry. We serve as the black box recorder for patients who have been ignored or dismissed.


The Methodology

Through public submissions and rigorous documentation, we track patterns of negligence, insurance chicanery, and the loss of humanity in healthcare.
